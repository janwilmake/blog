# The new hackers will impersonate the ones you love

Hacking is getting easier.

Developments https://www.youtube.com/watch?time_continue=86&v=ohmajJTcpNk in
AI provided tools for face and voice replication like never before.
Many tools we use nowadays don't have a strong verification to certify it's
really you that's acting. Emails and phone numbers, for one, are easy to be
replicated. As a malicious user, I can send content from any phone number or
email I want. Even calls.

In a few years, when these tools have grown up, they could be indistinguishable
from your real friends.

Measures
To counteract this, the tools we use will have to become safer. No more email.
No more calling with your old school phone number. All things where you act and
have to be trusted online, will have to be through verified platforms like
Facebook, Instagram, or Twitter.

Your friend in the example above? you may pay him instantly. But what you'll
have to do, is ask him if he'll ask you again via a Facebook call first. To
verify.
